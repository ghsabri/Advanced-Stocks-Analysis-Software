## üéØ KEY FEATURES EXPLAINED

### TR Indicator (Our Proprietary System)

**What it is:**  
A 6-stage classification system that identifies where a stock is in its market cycle, from Strong Buy to Strong Sell.

**The 6 Stages:**
- **Stage 1:** Basing/Accumulation (Neutral) - Stock building foundation
- **Stage 2:** Advancing (Buy) - Confirmed uptrend
- **Stage 2+:** Strong Advance (Strong Buy) ‚≠ê **BEST** - Powerful uptrend
- **Stage 3:** Topping/Distribution (Neutral) - Uptrend losing momentum
- **Stage 4:** Declining (Sell) - Confirmed downtrend
- **Stage 4+:** Strong Decline (Strong Sell) - Steep downtrend

**How to use it:**  
Focus on Stage 2 and Stage 2+ stocks for long positions. These represent stocks in confirmed uptrends with strong momentum.

**Color coding:**
- üü¢ **Green** = Bullish signals (Stage 2, 2+)
- üü° **Yellow** = Neutral (Stage 1, 3)
- üî¥ **Red** = Bearish signals (Stage 4, 4+)

---

### AI Confidence Scores

**What it is:**  
Machine learning models predict the success probability of detected chart patterns based on historical data.

**Confidence Levels:**
- **90-100%:** Very High (rare, strongest setups)
- **75-89%:** High (good probability of success)
- **60-74%:** Moderate (decent probability)
- **Below 60%:** Low (use caution)

**How to use it:**  
Combine with TR Indicator for best results. A Stage 2+ stock with an 80%+ confidence pattern is a high-probability trading setup.

**Model Accuracy:**
- Daily timeframe: **91.5% accuracy** (Ichimoku Cloud strategy)
- Weekly timeframe: **81.8% accuracy** (Ichimoku Cloud strategy)

**Continuation Patterns:**

**Breakout Patterns:**

**What you'll see for each pattern:**

- Timeframe for expected move
